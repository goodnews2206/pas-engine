# PAS — Master Context

## ORVN Labs

ORVN Labs is the company. It builds operational infrastructure for real estate brokerages. Its flagship product is **PAS**.

ORVN is an operator-led company, not a hype-driven startup. The posture is: build durable infrastructure that brokerages run their operations on, not a feature that sits on top of someone else's CRM. The brand voice (see `DANIEL_VOICE_AND_TONE.md`) is calm, analytical, and structural.

## What PAS is

**PAS is operational intelligence infrastructure for US real estate brokerages.**

PAS sits underneath a brokerage's operations and gives it three things it almost never has:

1. **Speed** — leads acted on in seconds, not hours.
2. **Continuity** — follow-up that does not depend on whether a human remembered.
3. **Memory** — a persistent operational record of the brokerage, its people, its leads, and its patterns (the **PAS Brain**).

PAS is the layer that knows what happened, what should happen next, and whether it actually happened. It is the brokerage's operational nervous system and long-term memory.

## What PAS is NOT

State this plainly and often, because the market will try to pattern-match PAS into a category it does not belong to:

- **PAS is not a CRM.** CRMs are databases of contacts that humans are supposed to maintain. PAS is the intelligence and memory layer that makes operations actually run. It can sit alongside a CRM, but it is not one.
- **PAS is not a chatbot.** It is not a conversational toy bolted onto a website. Conversation may be a surface; intelligence and memory are the substance.
- **PAS is not a social app.** No feeds, no vanity engagement. It is infrastructure.

## Why "infrastructure" and not "tool"

Tools are optional, swappable, and sit on the surface. Infrastructure is the thing the business runs *on* — when it works, no one notices; when it fails, everything stops. PAS is positioned as infrastructure: the operational substrate of a brokerage, not another app in the stack. This distinction is central to both product and content strategy.

## Delivery posture

- **Web-first.** The product is delivered through the web today.
- **Becoming a Progressive Web App (PWA).** Installable, offline-tolerant, push-capable — without an app-store dependency.
- **Native mobile is deferred.** It is not abandoned; it is explicitly *later*. Do not assume native iOS/Android work is in scope. (See `PAS_PRODUCT_ARCHITECTURE.md`.)

## The core problem PAS solves

US real estate brokerages leak money through their operations, not their marketing:

- Leads arrive and sit (slow speed-to-lead).
- Leads fall through cracks (lead leakage).
- Inside Sales Agents / ISA functions underperform or churn (ISA failure).
- Follow-up is inconsistent and human-dependent.
- No one can see whether agents actually did what they were supposed to (accountability + operational visibility).
- The brokerage has no memory — every departure, every handoff, every "who talked to this lead last?" resets to zero.

PAS exists to close those gaps structurally, not with more reminders or more dashboards that humans must babysit.
